Inspiration

My forebears migrated from Germany to Australia in the 1800's. They were farmers, and they lived off the land, which provided them everything they needed.

The old ways, of making jam, chutney, pickles, preserving vegetables and fruits, are foreign to many in this age. But the ways are still there, and this site is a way to record the events of your garden, each year improving on the last, learning how to work with, and get the most out of nature.

Every journey starts with the first step. Register, start adding your plants, your recurring garden events, and those notes become the ground for knowing better next year.
